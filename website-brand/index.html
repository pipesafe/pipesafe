Usage

Do

  • Maintain at least the height of one facet as clear space around the mark.
  • Use the white-on-dark variant on backgrounds darker than #001E2B.
  • Pair the mark with the “PipeSafe” wordmark in JetBrains Mono.
  • Render at 24px or larger so the three facets remain legible.

Don’t

  • Recolor the facets. The three colors are the brand.
  • Rotate, skew, or distort the mark.
  • Place the color mark on a background tinted near any of the facet colors.
  • Add drop shadows or outer glows.
